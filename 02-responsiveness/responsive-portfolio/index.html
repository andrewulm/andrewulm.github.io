About Me

Hi. I'm Andrew.

I'm a (soon to be) Full-Stack Web Developer

I'm currently a Junior Developer and Designer at an E-Commerce company, making new components and pages on an Angular 4 application to market and sell as much product as humanly possible.

I've been creating websites since the glory days of Angelfire and Geocities, making "awesome" Dragonball Z fan pages in my youth. I taught myself how to code HTML and CSS with notepad back then and I've grown my skills to be a semi-dangerous UX/UI master, ripping apart pretty wire frames and making them functional on the web.

It's time for me to go "Super Saiyan" and level up my skills to adapt to a rapidly changing web development community by adding Javascript, MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js to my tool belt.

I'm a handy man around the house, I have a lovely girlfriend, a beautiful daughter, and a pretty rad pupper named Riley. I love solving problems, so all four of those give me ample opportunity to put my problem solving skills to the test.
